About Sky Electric

Sky Electric is an Okotoks based electrical contractor, servicing the Okotoks, High River, and Calgary areas. Created by Cody Muri, a Master Electrician with 10+ years of experience etched across work. Offering services in residential, commerical, retrofitting, renovations, AV systems and home automation, Sky Electric is well equipped to handle a myriad of client needs with confidence.

Sky Electric draws its integrity from the value and effort it invests in:

  • The importance of transparent communication with the clients, building strong relationships through trust and mutual respect.
  • Master level quality of work, performed methodically, precisely and efficiently.
  • Maintaining a clean and mindful workspace, taking care to get the job in the least intrusive manner possible.
