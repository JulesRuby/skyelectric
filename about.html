History

Who are we?

Sky Electric is an Okotoks based electrical company, servicing the Okotoks, High River, and Calgary areas. Created by Cody Muri, a Master Electrician with 10+ years of experience etched across his work. Offering services in residential, commercial, retrofitting, renovations, AV systems and home automation, Sky Electric is well equipped to handle a myriad of client needs with confidence.

Core Values

Experience guides our hand, values inform our business

  1. Communication

    We believe in maintaining punctual, transparent communication with our clients from the start. By reaching clear understanding with you from the start, we ensure that you feel comfortable and happy knowing what to expect. Nothing should be left to your imagination when it comes to the final product. If the situation necessitates a change in course, we immediately let you know before moving ahead.

  2. Pride

    Taking pride in our operations is what keeps us at the top of our game. Every job is an opportunity to outperform the last, and commitment to continuous improvement fulfills our commitment to customer satisfaction. If you can’t take pride in what you’re doing, you’re doing the wrong thing.

  3. Respect

    Sky Electric strives to create a respectful work environment on the job. We take pride in keeping a clean and respectful work area, leaving as little of trace as possible behind throughout. Nobody wants to feel uncomfortable comfortable in their space, whether it’s their home, office, shop, you name it. Sky Electric is a guest, and our goal is to ultimately leave it better than it used to be, not worse.
